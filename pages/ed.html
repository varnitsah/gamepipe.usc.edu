Games Education

USC covers the entire spectrum of education with respect to game development. If you are engineering-oriented, gameplay design-focused, or artistic, we have a degree program for you!

Game degree programs at USC include:

  • BS in Computer Science (Games)
  • MS in Computer Science (Game Development)
  • PhD in Computer Science (Games)
  • BA in Interactive Entertainment
  • MFA in Interactive Media
  • 2D Game Art & Design Minor
  • 3D Game Art & Design Minor
