Advanced Game Projects Fall 2017 - Spring 2018
08/31/2017 09:50

Seven advanced games projects have been selected for CSCI-529A & B for Fall 2017 & Spring 2018. The games selected are the following:

- Embedded
- Fan the Cannon
- Friendshrimp
- Knight Errant
- Supernova
- Unfolded
- Wavebent

Information about these games is <a href="https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/0B6gY4d5XJcs1cmRqeVNyclFaTWs" rel="external" target="_blank">here</a>.

Syllabus is <a href="http://gamepipe.usc.edu/finalgames/syllabus.php" rel="external" target="_blank">here</a>.

If you are interested in becoming part of one of these teams, please come to the first class on Thursday the 24th of August 2017 at 3:30pm to see the game design presentations in EGG-108!
