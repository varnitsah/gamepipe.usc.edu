USC GamePipe Laboratory Celebrates the Tenth Anniversary of the Founding of the Computer Science Games Program
06/15/2016
The USC Viterbi School of Engineering invites you to the Tenth Anniversary Celebration of the Founding of the Computer Science Games Program & the GamePipe Laboratory, Wednesday the 15th of June 2016, 7pm to 10pm.

On the occasion of the week of E3, the purpose of this event is to bring together alumni of CS Games as well as of the GamePipe Laboratory & all those who have built games in CS in celebration of the 10th anniversary of their founding.
